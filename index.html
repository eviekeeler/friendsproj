About Friends

Friends is an American Sitcom that about 6 friends living in New York as they navigate their way through life and learn to grow up as they approach the third decade of their life. Friends has become one of the most popular shows of all times and is still being watched by many ages today. The main characters names are Racehl, Chandler, Monica, Joey, Phoebe, and Joey.
